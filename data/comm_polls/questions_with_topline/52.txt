Kaiser Health Tracking Poll [December, 2014]

(I'm going to read you a list of specific elements or parts of the (2010 health reform) law. For each, please tell me whether you think it is something the law does, or not.) To the best of your knowledge, would you say the health reform law does or does not...require employers with 100 or more employees to pay a fine if they don't offer health insurance starting in January 2015?

69%	Yes, law does this
16%	No, law does not do this
15%	Don't know/Refused

Survey by Henry J. Kaiser Family Foundation
Methodology: Conducted by Princeton Survey Research Associates International December 2-December 9, 2014, and based on telephone interviews with a national adult sample of 1,505. 750 respondents were interviewed on a landline telephone, and 755 were interviewed on a cell phone, including 421 who had no landline telephone.
