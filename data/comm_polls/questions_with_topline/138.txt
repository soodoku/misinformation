Pew Research Center for the People & the Press Poll [February, 2015]

Thinking about vaccines for diseases such as measles, mumps, and rubella, do you think these vaccines are safe for healthy children or are they not safe for healthy children?

83%	Safe for healthy children
9%	Not safe for healthy children
7%	Don't know/Refused

Survey by Pew Research Center for the People & the Press
Methodology: Conducted by Princeton Survey Research Associates International February 5-February 8, 2015, and based on telephone interviews with a national adult sample of 1,003. 501 respondents were interviewed on a landline telephone, and 502 were interviewed on a cell phone, including 300 who had no landline telephone. [USPSRA.020915.R04]
Dataset: USPEW2015-WK0205
Data provided by The Roper Center for Public Opinion Research.
