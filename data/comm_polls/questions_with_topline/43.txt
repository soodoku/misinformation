Kaiser Health Tracking Poll [March, 2015]

(I'm going to read you a list of specific elements or parts of the law. For each, please tell me whether you think it is something the law does, or not.) To the best of your knowledge, would you say the (2010) health reform law does or does not...provide financial help to low and moderate income Americans who don't get insurance through their jobs to help them purchase coverage?

65%	Yes, law does this
29%	No, law does not do this
6%	Don't know/Refused

Survey by Henry J. Kaiser Family Foundation
Methodology: Conducted by Princeton Survey Research Associates International March 6-March 13, 2015, and based on telephone interviews with a national adult sample of 1,503. 600 respondents were interviewed on a landline telephone, and 903 were interviewed on a cell phone, including 498 who had no landline telephone.
