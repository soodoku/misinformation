PEW RESEARCH CENTER FOR THE PEOPLE 
FEBRUARY 2007 POLITICAL KNOWLEDGE SURVEY 
WHAT AMERICANS KNOW: 1989-2007
February 1-13, 2007
N= 1502


This report includes data from a second survey conducted March 9-12, 2007 that tested closed-ended versions of some of the open-ended knowledge items on the main February study.  This secondary datafile is called "Knowledge Omnibus March07c.sav".  The data was collected as part of the ORC/Caravan ominbus survey.
