Princeton Survey Research Associates International/Newsweek Poll [August, 2007]

Do you think most climate scientists agree that human activities, such as burning coal and oil, are a major cause of global warming, or do you think there is a lot of disagreement among climate scientists about whether human activities are a major cause?

47%	Most scientists agree
42%	A lot of disagreement
12%	Don't know/Refused

Survey by Newsweek
Methodology: Conducted by Princeton Survey Research Associates International August 1-August 2, 2007, and based on telephone interviews with a national adult sample of 1,002.  [USPSRNEW.07NW11.Q15]
Dataset: USPSRA2007-NW11
Variable: Q15
Data provided by The Roper Center for Public Opinion Research.
