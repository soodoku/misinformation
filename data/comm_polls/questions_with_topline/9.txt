PIPA/Knowledge Networks Poll [November, 2010]

As you may know, some people have suggested that President (Barack) Obama was not born in the United States.  Do you think Obama was not born in the US, Obama was born in the US, or it is not clear whether Obama was born in the US or not?

15%	Obama was not born in the US
55%	Obama was born in the US
29%	It is not clear whether Obama was born in the US or not
1%	Don't know/Refused

Methodology: Conducted by Program On International Policy Attitudes, University of Maryland November 6-November 15, 2010, and based on online interviews with a national adult sample of 848. The poll was fielded by Knowledge Networks using its national panel which is randomly selected from the entire national population and subsequently provided Internet access, if needed. [USUMARY.120910.R35]
Dataset: USUMARY2010-11
Data provided by The Roper Center for Public Opinion Research.
