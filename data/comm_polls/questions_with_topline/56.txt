Kaiser Health Tracking Poll [October, 2014]

To the best of your knowledge, would you say the (2010) health reform law does or does not provide financial help to low and moderate income Americans who don't get insurance through their jobs to help them purchase coverage?

54%	Yes, law provides
31%	No, does not provide
16%	Don't know/Refused

Survey by Henry J. Kaiser Family Foundation
Methodology: Conducted by Princeton Survey Research Associates International October 8-October 14, 2014, and based on telephone interviews with a national adult sample of 1,503. 751 respondents were interviewed on a landline telephone, and 752 were interviewed on a cell phone, including 411 who had no landline telephone. [USPSRA.102114AK.R10]
Data provided by The Roper Center for Public Opinion Research.
