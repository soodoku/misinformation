Kaiser Health Tracking Poll [January, 2014]

(I'm going to read you a list of specific elements or parts of the (2010 health reform) law. For each, please tell me whether you think it is something the law does, or not.) First, to the best of your knowledge, would you say the health reform law does or does not...give states the option of expanding their existing Medicaid program to cover more low-income, uninsured adults?

58%	Yes, law does this
27%	No, law does not do this
15%	Don't know/Refused

Survey by Henry J. Kaiser Family Foundation
Methodology: Conducted by Princeton Survey Research Associates International January 14-January 21, 2014, and based on telephone interviews with a national adult sample of 1,506. 753 respondents were interviewed on a landline telephone, and 753 were interviewed on a cell phone, including 402 who had no landline telephone.
