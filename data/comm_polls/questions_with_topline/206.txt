Associated Press/Gfk Knowledge Networks Poll [August, 2012]

Where was Barack Obama born, as far as you know? (If No answer, ask:) Is it your best guess that Obama was born in the United States, or in another country?

49%	In the United States
39%	In another country
12%	Refused/Not answered

Survey by Associated Press
Methodology: Conducted by Gfk Knowledge Networks August 30-September 11, 2012, and based on online interviews with a national adult sample of 1,071. The poll was fielded by Gfk Knowledge Networks using its panel which is randomly selected from the entire national population and subsequently provided Internet access, if needed. [USAP.102712G.R47]
Data provided by The Roper Center for Public Opinion Research.
