Kaiser Health Tracking Poll [January, 2015]

(I'm going to read you a list of specific elements or parts of the law. For each, please tell me whether you think it is something the law does, or not.) To the best of your knowledge, would you say the (2010) health reform law does or does not...require employers with 100 or more employees to pay a fine if they don't offer health insurance starting in January 2015?

67%	Yes, law does this
19%	No, law does not do this
13%	Don't know/Refused

Survey by Henry J. Kaiser Family Foundation
Methodology: Conducted by Princeton Survey Research Associates International January 15-January 21, 2015, and based on telephone interviews with a national adult sample of 1,503. 751 respondents were interviewed on a landline telephone, and 752 were interviewed on a cell phone, including 450 who had no landline telephone.
