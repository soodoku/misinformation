Fox News Poll [May, 2017]

Do you think Russia's attempts to influence the (2016) presidential election using cyber-attacks helped Donald Trump or helped Hillary Clinton, or do you think they had no real effect? Do you think Russia helped Clinton/Trump a lot or just a little?

30%	Helped Trump-A lot
14%	Helped Trump-A little
49%	No real effect
1%	Helped Clinton-A lot
1%	Helped Clinton-A little
6%	Don't know

Survey by Fox News
Methodology: Conducted by Anderson Robbins Research/Shaw & Co. Research May 21-May 23, 2017, and based on telephone interviews with a national registered voters sample of 1,011. 419 respondents were interviewed on a landline telephone, and 592 were interviewed on a cell phone. Interviews were conducted by Braun Research. [USASFOX.052417.R24]
Data provided by The Roper Center for Public Opinion Research.
