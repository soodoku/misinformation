Fox News Poll [December, 2016]

Do you think Russia's attempts to influence the (2016) presidential election using cyber-attacks helped Donald Trump or helped Hillary Clinton, or do you think they had no real effect? Do you think Russia  helped Clinton/Trump a lot or just a little?

21%	Helped Trump a lot
11%	Helped Trump a little
59%	No real effect
1%	Helped Clinton a lot
*%	Helped Clinton a little
7%	Don't know

Subpopulation: Asked of partial sample

Survey by Fox News
Methodology: Conducted by Anderson Robbins Research/Shaw & Co. Research December 11-December 13, 2016, and based on telephone interviews with a national registered voters sample of 1,034. 426 respondents were interviewed on a landline telephone, and 608 were interviewed on a cell phone. Interviews were conducted by Braun Research. [USASFOX.121416.R52]
Data provided by The Roper Center for Public Opinion Research.
