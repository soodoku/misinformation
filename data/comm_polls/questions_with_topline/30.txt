Associated Press/Yahoo Poll [August, 2008]

Do you happen to know the religion of each of the following (2008 presidential election) candidates?  If you don't know, you can mark that too....Protestant, Catholic, Mormon, Jewish, Muslim, some other religion, no religion...Barack Obama

36%	Protestant
4%	Catholic
14%	Muslim
5%	Some other religion
1%	No religion
38%	Don't know
1%	Refused/Not answered

Survey by Associated Press, Yahoo
Methodology: Conducted by Knowledge Networks August 27-September 5, 2008, and based on online interviews with a national adult sample of 2,227. The poll was fielded by Knowledge Networks using its national panel which is randomly selected from the entire national population and subsequently provided Internet access, if needed.  The poll was done in partnership with Stanford University. [USAP.092308.R27A]
Data provided by The Roper Center for Public Opinion Research.
