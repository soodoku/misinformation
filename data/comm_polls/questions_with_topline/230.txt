Fox News Poll [June, 2017]

Do you think Russia's attempts to influence the (2016) presidential election using cyberattacks helped Donald Trump or helped Hillary Clinton, or do you think they had no real effect? Do you think Russia helped Clinton/Trump a lot or just a little?

29%	Helped Trump-A lot
14%	Helped Trump-A little
50%	No real effect
1%	Helped Clinton-A lot
1%	Helped Clinton-A little
5%	Don't know

Survey by Fox News
Methodology: Conducted by Anderson Robbins Research/Shaw & Co. Research June 25-June 27, 2017, and based on telephone interviews with a national registered voters sample of 1,017. 426 respondents were interviewed on a landline telephone, and 591 were interviewed on a cell phone. Interviews were conducted by Braun Research. [USASFOX.062817.R39]
Data provided by The Roper Center for Public Opinion Research.
