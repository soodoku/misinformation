Associated Press/Gfk Knowledge Networks Poll [August, 2012]

Do you happen to know the religion of each of the following people?  If you don't know, you can mark that too....Protestant, Catholic, Mormon, Jewish, Muslim, some other religion, no religion...Barack Obama

28%	Protestant
5%	Catholic
18%	Muslim
10%	Some other religion
2%	No religion
35%	Don't know
2%	Refused/Not answered

Survey by Associated Press
Methodology: Conducted by Gfk Knowledge Networks August 30-September 11, 2012, and based on online interviews with a national adult sample of 1,071. The poll was fielded by Gfk Knowledge Networks using its panel which is randomly selected from the entire national population and subsequently provided Internet access, if needed.
