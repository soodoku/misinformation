Kaiser Health Tracking Poll [September, 2013]

(I'm going to read you a list of specific elements or parts of the law.  For each, please tell me whether you think it is included in the (2010) health reform law, or not.) To the best of your knowledge, would you say the health reform law does or does not...allow undocumented immigrants to receive financial help from the government to buy health insurance?

43%	Yes, law does this
35%	No, law does not do this
21%	Don't know/Refused
