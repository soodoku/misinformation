Kaiser Health Tracking Poll [March, 2015]

As you may know, the (2010 health reform) law requires nearly all Americans to have health insurance or else pay a fine when they file their taxes. As far as you know, did the requirement to report your health insurance status on your tax return take effect last year, that is for filing 2013 taxes, this year, that is for filing 2014 taxes, or does it take effect next year for 2015 taxes?

11%	Last year, 2013 taxes
53%	This year, 2014 taxes
20%	Next year, 2015 taxes
1%	Some other year (Vol.)
16%	Don't know/Refused

Survey by Henry J. Kaiser Family Foundation
Methodology: Conducted by Princeton Survey Research Associates International March 6-March 13, 2015, and based on telephone interviews with a national adult sample of 1,503. 600 respondents were interviewed on a landline telephone, and 903 were interviewed on a cell phone, including 498 who had no landline telephone.
