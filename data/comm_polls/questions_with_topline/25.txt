Associated Press/Knowledge Networks Poll [December, 2009]

Do you happen to know the religion of each of the following people?  If you don't know, you can mark that too....Protestant, Catholic, Mormon, Jewish, Muslim, some other religion, no religion...Barack Obama

26%	Protestant
4%	Catholic
17%	Muslim
8%	Some other religion
2%	No religion
41%	Don't know
1%	Refused/Not answered

Survey by Associated Press
Methodology: Conducted by Knowledge Networks December 23-January 10, 2010, and based on online interviews with a national adult sample of 1,037. The poll was fielded by Knowledge Networks using its national panel which is randomly selected from the entire national population and subsequently provided Internet access, if needed.
