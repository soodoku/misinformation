Washington Post Poll [April, 2011]

(Where was Barack Obama born, as far as you know?) (If Don't know, ask:) Is it your best guess that Obama was born in the United States, or in another country? (If Another country, ask:) Do you think there's been solid evidence of that, or is that your suspicion only?

77%	United States
8%	United States-best guess
1%	Another country-solid evidence
8%	Another country-suspicion only
1%	Another country-no opinion
4%	No opinion

Methodology: Conducted by Washington Post April 28-May 1, 2011, and based on telephone interviews with a national adult sample of 1,006. Interviews were conducted by landline and cell phones.  Sampling, data collection and tabulation by Princeton Survey Research Associates International. [USWASHP.050511WP.R3]
Data provided by The Roper Center for Public Opinion Research.
