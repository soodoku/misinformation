Princeton Survey Research Associates International/Newsweek Poll [August, 2007]

Now, thinking specifically about the issue of global warming...do you think most climate scientists agree that the earth has been warming in recent years, or do you think there is a lot of disagreement among climate scientists about whether the earth has been warming?

52%	Most scientists agree
39%	A lot of disagreement
9%	Don't know/Refused

Survey by Newsweek
Methodology: Conducted by Princeton Survey Research Associates International August 1-August 2, 2007, and based on telephone interviews with a national adult sample of 1,002.  [USPSRNEW.07NW11.Q14]
