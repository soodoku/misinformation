Pew Research Center for the People & the Press Political/Foreign Policy Poll [September, 2008]

Now, thinking about Barack Obama's religious beliefs...Do you happen to know what Barack Obama's religion is?  Is he Christian, Jewish, Muslim, Buddhist, Hindu, atheist, agnostic, or something else? (If Don't know, probe:) Is that because you've heard different things about his religion, or because you just don't know enough about him?

4%	Something else

Subpopulation: Asked of Form 1 half sample

Survey by Pew Research Center for the People & the Press
